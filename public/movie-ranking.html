The Best TV Shows According to Numan

My top 3 TV shows of all time

The Blacklist

One of the best crime drama. Created around the life of the most wanted criminal Raymond Reddington known as the concierge of crime.

The White Collar

TV show based on the life of Neal Caffrey, the greatest con man.

Special Ops

TV show revolving around an Indian spy Himmat Singh and his team.
