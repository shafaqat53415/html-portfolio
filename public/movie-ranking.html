The Best Movies or TV Shows According to Shafaqat Numan

My Top 3 Movies of all-time

Kabhi Haan Kabhi Naa

"Kabhi Haan Kabhi Naa" is a romantic comedy about a love-struck young man, Sunil, who struggles with unrequited love and life choices, learning valuable lessons about acceptance and self-discovery.

Cars

"Cars" is an animated adventure about Lightning McQueen, a hotshot race car who discovers the true meaning of friendship and life beyond the finish line when he finds himself stranded in a small town.

Top Gun

"Top Gun" is an action-packed film about a talented but reckless Navy pilot, Maverick, who competes to be the best at an elite fighter school while facing personal and professional challenges.



My Top 3 TV Shows of all-time

The Blacklist

"The Blacklist" follows Raymond "Red" Reddington, a notorious criminal who mysteriously surrenders to the FBI, offering to help capture high-profile criminals on his "blacklist" in exchange for working exclusively with rookie profiler Elizabeth Keen.

Monk

"Monk" is a detective series about Adrian Monk, a brilliant former police detective with obsessive-compulsive disorder, who uses his unique abilities to solve crimes while struggling with his personal challenges.

White Collar

"White Collar" follows the unlikely partnership between a charming con artist, Neal Caffrey, and an FBI agent, Peter Burke, as they team up to catch white-collar criminals while navigating a world of deception and trust.
